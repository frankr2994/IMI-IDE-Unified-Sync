# Scout's Woodland Discovery

Scout's nose was glued to the damp earth as he wove through the towering pines. The woods were alive with the scent of pine needles and the distant chatter of a squirrel. He bounded over a fallen log, his ears flopping rhythmically, until he stumbled upon a sparkling stream cutting through a mossy glade. After a long, cool drink, he settled into a patch of sunlight, the king of his own quiet wilderness.
